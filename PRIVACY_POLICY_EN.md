# Etherea — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 9, 2026

## 1. General Information

Etherea is a mystical application for astrology, numerology, Tarot, and the lunar calendar. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## 2. What Data We Collect

The App stores the following data **only on your device and in your personal iCloud**:

- **Name and Gender** — for numerological calculations, personalization, and AI forecasts.
- **Date of Birth** — for astrological and numerological calculations.
- **Time of Birth** (optional) — for Ascendant calculation.
- **Birthplace and Current Location** — for determining coordinates (astrological calculations).
- **Diary Entries** — your personal notes and AI analysis.
- **Wish List** — your desires, intentions, and AI analysis.
- **Collection and Achievements** — collected cards, runes, crystals, and Initiation steps.

## 3. Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI)

The App actively uses Artificial Intelligence technologies (via aggregator platforms based on the OpenAI API, for example, ProxyAPI / DeepSeek) for personalized forecasts, Tarot readings, and the Magic Ball.

**Data Transfer:**
When pressing AI interaction buttons (and only at that moment), the App sends your current context via an encrypted channel (HTTPS):
- Name, gender, date, time, and place of birth.
- Current geographic location.
- Query context (drawn Tarot cards, question to the "Magic Ball", a diary entry limited to 100 words, or the text of your wish).

**AI Privacy:**
API providers agree not to use the data provided by users to train their neural networks. We do not store your questions and AI answers on our servers.

## 4. Sharing Data with Third Parties

With the exception of strictly necessary data transfer to AI servers to generate text in real time, **We DO NOT share or sell your data to third parties for marketing or analytics.**

- No tracking
- No hidden ads
- No user databases (only your iCloud is used)

## 5. Data Storage

- All data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted form (using SwiftData).
- If iCloud is enabled, data is synchronized to your personal iCloud account.
- Analytical data regarding App crashes (Crashlytics) is used exclusively for fixing bugs, if you have allowed it in iOS settings.

## 6. Location Access

The App may request location access **only for astronomical calculations and to auto-fill the current city**. Your location is NOT constantly tracked in the background, and we do not store your location history.

## 7. Data Deletion

You are the full owner of your data. You can delete all your data at any time:
- Via the App settings (Reset profile).
- By deleting the App from your device and deleting the Etherea backup in your Apple ID (iCloud) settings.

## 8. Children

The App does not contain age restrictions but is not specifically intended for young children.

## 9. Changes to the Policy

In the event of significant changes to the policy (e.g., integrating new AI services), we will notify users through an App update.
